ABOUT SSBCI

State Small Business Credit Initiative

In 2020, the US Treasury renewed SSBCI 2.0 with a $10B budget. The program is administered by States, DC, territories, and Tribal governments. This includes venture capital programs, loan participation programs, loan guarantee programs, collateral support programs, and capital access programs. Funds are released in tranches based on demonstrated outcomes.

  • US Treasury allocates funds based on State population, Tribal allocations separately.
  • SSBCI allocates funds for Venture Capital programs in a supplemental form to existing capital, requires proof of impact.
  • Program requires fund compliance and reporting on job creation, underserved communities, and fund dispersion.
